Our Story

At Aegis IT Solutions, we believe technology should make your life easier, not harder. Based in Cedartown, GA, we are a family-owned business dedicated to providing honest, jargon-free IT support to our neighbors and local businesses.


We saw a need in our community for reliable tech support that doesn't cost a fortune. Whether you need a simple virus removal or a full business network installation, we treat every client like family.

Our Core Values

Honesty First

We will never sell you hardware or software you don't need. We provide transparent assessments and quotes before starting any work.

When your tech breaks, you need help fast. We prioritize quick turnaround times and offer same-day service for emergencies.

Community Focused
